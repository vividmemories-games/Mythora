## Purpose

Why Mythora exists and what it must become. Mechanics live in [GAMEPLAY.md](../GAMEPLAY.md); this document states intent and non-goals.

## Elevator pitch

**Mythora** is a turn-based puzzle RPG: match tiles to generate **resources**, spend **AP** on hero skills, and survive enemy turns. The board never deals damage directly.

## Pillars

1. **Tactical matching** — every turn is a resource/AP decision, not “biggest combo wins.”
2. **Systems over art** — reusable battle UI, data-driven heroes/enemies, manageable art for a solo developer.
3. **Distinct identity** — dusk adventure (teal/ink + amber/gold), not Dot Clash neon and not Labyrinth Legends stone-citadel.
4. **Phased live-service** — campaign + daily first; Royal Match–style events later without copying live-ops sprawl.
5. **Firebase-safe later** — mock/local until the loop feels good; then server-validated economy.

## Audience

Mobile puzzle + light RPG players who like short tactical battles and collecting heroes.

## Non-goals (near term)

- Pay-to-win PvP
- Large handcrafted maps / cinematic environments
- Multi-hero parties before Phase 3–4
- Full Royal Match event calendar in MVP
- Shipping Firebase before the battle loop is fun

## Success for Phase 1

A player can finish a stub battle and feel the loop: match → resources/AP → skill → enemy response — with cascades and readable motion.

## Naming

Product name is **Mythora** (formerly working titles Relicbound / design-doc aliases). Code, store, and docs should prefer Mythora.
